About RentLedger

RentLedger is a rent and tenant management application designed for landlords and property owners. The platform helps users manage tenant records, rent collection tracking, maintenance requests, agreement information, reminders, and related property management activities.

User Data

  • Tenant names
  • Property names
  • Room numbers
  • Rent amounts
  • Security deposit information
  • Lease information
  • Maintenance records
  • Notes entered by users

Technical Information

  • Device information

  • Application version

How We Use Information

  • Provide application functionality

  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Provide customer support

Payments

Payments are processed by third-party providers such as Razorpay. RentLedger does not store debit card numbers, credit card numbers, UPI PINs, banking passwords, or similar payment credentials.

Data Storage

Data may be stored and processed using cloud service providers including Supabase and associated infrastructure providers.

Data Sharing

RentLedger does not sell user information. Information may be shared only with service providers required to operate the application, payment processors, authentication providers, or where required by law.
