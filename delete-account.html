Delete Your RentLedger Account

RentLedger allows users to request permanent deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: 3billions@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the Google account used to sign in to RentLedger.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Tenant records
  • Property information
  • Rent collection records
  • Maintenance records
  • Application preferences

Retention Period

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. After deletion, personal account data is permanently removed from our systems.

For questions, contact: 3billions@gmail.com
