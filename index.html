RentLedger

RentLedger is a simple rent and tenant management application designed for small landlords.

Features

  • Track tenant details
  • Manage monthly rent payments
  • Monitor pending and overdue rent
  • Agreement expiry reminders
  • Maintenance request tracking
  • Tenant notes and records
  • Google Sign-In authentication
